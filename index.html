About Me

I am a web developer. I live and work in Knoxville, TN. I have worked remotely for eight years. I work in my office in the Bearden part of Knoxville which I share with my Border Collie, Malcolm. I work with businesses of every size and technologies spanning the trends of the past fifteen years in software development. I spent the majority of my carreer in consulting, but I have also worked on medium to large teams while employed. I developed a graphical resume so that you can see a quick overview of the scope of my experience in software development.
